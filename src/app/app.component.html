    About Us

    Seceon is laser focused on detecting and eradicating cyber security threats in real-time. Seceon's approach is to apply advanced data collection and analysis, machine learning and patent-pending predictive behavioral analytics to detect threats –Businesses and organizations have seen rampant and unstoppable computer network security breaches become everyday news.
